### Note on the Pope source (honest provenance)

The task names **G. U. Pope (1886)** on sacred-texts.com /tamil/tku/ as the English source. That site is served behind a Cloudflare interstitial and is not directly fetchable. The identical text -- *Tirukkural: English Translation and Commentary* by Rev. Dr. G. U. Pope, Rev. W. H. Drew, Rev. John Lazarus and F. W. Ellis, W. H. Allen & Co., **1886** -- is bundled (Tamil + Pope English + prose commentary) inside `libkural.py` of the public **tshrinivasan/libkural** repository, where each couplet is an explicit `Kural.factory(no, pal, adhikaram, tamil, english, commentary)` record. We parse that table for `text_en` (Pope) and `original` (Tamil). Pope died in 1908 and the work was first published in 1886, so the translation is firmly public domain. Reference edition: Rev. Dr. G. U. Pope et al., *Tirukkural: English Translation and Commentary*, W. H. Allen & Co., 1886 (public domain); digitised at https://www.sacred-texts.com/tamil/tku/

### Kural 1  (chapter 1 -- கடவுள் வாழ்த்து; section Aram (Virtue); tokens=19)

- **original (Tamil):** அகர முதல எழுத்தெல்லாம் ஆதி பகவன் முதற்றே உலகு.
- **transliteration:** Akara Mudhala Ezhuththellaam Aadhi Pakavan Mudhatre Ulaku
- **text_en (Pope 1886):** A, as its first of letters, every speech maintains; The 'Primal Deity' is first through all the world's domains.

### Kural 47  (chapter 5 -- இல்வாழ்க்கை; section Aram (Virtue); tokens=19)

- **original (Tamil):** இயல்பினான் இல்வாழ்க்கை வாழ்பவன் என்பான் முயல்வாருள் எல்லாம் தலை.
- **transliteration:** Iyalpinaan Ilvaazhkkai Vaazhpavan Enpaan Muyalvaarul Ellaam Thalai
- **text_en (Pope 1886):** In nature's way who spends his calm domestic days, 'Mid all that strive for virtue's crown hath foremost place.

### Kural 391  (chapter 40 -- கல்வி; section Porul (Wealth); tokens=18)

- **original (Tamil):** கற்க கசடறக் கற்பவை கற்றபின் நிற்க அதற்குத் தக.
- **transliteration:** Karka Kasatarak Karpavai Katrapin Nirka Adharkuth Thaka
- **text_en (Pope 1886):** So learn that you may full and faultless learning gain, Then in obedience meet to lessons learnt remain.

### Kural 1081  (chapter 109 -- தகையணங்குறுத்தல்; section Inbam (Love); tokens=22)

- **original (Tamil):** அணங்குகொல் ஆய்மயில் கொல்லோ கனங்குழை மாதர்கொல் மாலும்என் நெஞ்சு.
- **transliteration:** Anangukol Aaimayil Kollo Kananguzhai Maadharkol Maalum En Nenju
- **text_en (Pope 1886):** Goddess? or peafowl rare? She whose ears rich jewels wear, Is she a maid of human kind? All wildered is my mind!

### Kural 1330  (chapter 133 -- ஊடலுவகை; section Inbam (Love); tokens=18)

- **original (Tamil):** ஊடுதல் காமத்திற்கு இன்பம் அதற்கின்பம் கூடி முயங்கப் பெறின்.
- **transliteration:** Ootudhal Kaamaththirku Inpam Adharkinpam Kooti Muyangap Perin
- **text_en (Pope 1886):** A 'feigned aversion' coy to pleasure gives a zest; The pleasure's crowned when breast is clasped to breast.
